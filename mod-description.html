Adds cumulative production charts to the Stats screen's Resource Production section. Every yield gets a "Rate" sub-tab (the original per-turn chart) and a new "Cumulative" sub-tab showing total production over time.

Features

  • Every yield (Growth, Civics, Training, Culture, Happiness, Science, Money, Orders, Food, Iron, Stone, Wood) gets interleaved "Rate" and "Cumulative" sub-tabs
  • Cumulative charts show total production accumulated over the course of the game
  • Works in both single-player and multiplayer
  • No gameplay changes — purely informational
